  1. Sanitize all equipment
  2. Heat water to between 140 - 155 degrees
  3. Stir in malt aka mashing in
  4. Stir mixture occasionally and mash between 60 and 120 minutes
  5. Raise tempreture to 168-170 degrees and hold for 10 minutes, mashing out
  6. Strain the grain and the wort into a tall pot
  7. Pour at least a half a gallon of water over the grain and into the pot with wort aka sparging
  8. Bring wort to boil
  9. Enter the hot break zone, stir constantly to prevent the wort from overflowing
  10. Add hops and other flavorings according to your recipie
  11. Boil for at least 60 minutes
  12. Close to flameout, fill your sink with water and ice
  13. At flameout put pot with wort in the ice bath
  14. Cool wort to at least 70 degrees
  15. Pour wort into fermenter
  16. Pitch your yeast
  17. Shake fermenter vigurously
  18. Install blowout tube in fermenter and place other side of tube in a glass with sanitizer
  19. Store in a dark room in the tempreature range indicated on your yeast
  20. After two days, replace blowoff tube with airlock
  21. Ferment for two weeks
  22. Bottle beer
  23. Carbonate for two weeks
  24. Enjoy!
